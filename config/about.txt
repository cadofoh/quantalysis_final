About Quantitative Risk Analysis Tool:

The Quantitative Risk Analysis Tool is a software application designed to assist in the quantitative assessment of risks associated with critical infrastructure systems.

It allows users to input various parameters related to different critical infrastructure sectors, such as power grids, transportation systems, and financial institutions, to analyse and evaluate potential risks.

This tool is developed as part of a project aimed at enhancing the resilience of critical infrastructure systems against various threats and hazards.
It leverages multiple quantitative risk analysis techniques to provide insights into the impact of different risk scenarios.
